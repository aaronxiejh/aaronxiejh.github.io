Bio



Jiaheng Xie is a fourth-year Ph.D. candidate in the Management Information Systems Department at the University of Arizona. He is expected to graduate in May 2020.

His research interests lie in business analytics and health risk analytics. His research aims to develop fine-grained deep learning-based analytical framework to understand, predict, and mitigate health risks from three progressive levels: patient behavior risk, disease risk, and intervention risk.

His prior works have been published or under review at many premier journals, including MIS Quarterly, Information Systems Research, and Journal of American Medical Informatics Association (JAMIA). Jiaheng Xie has also presented his work at many top conferences, including International Conference on Information Systems (ICIS) and Conference on Information Systems and Technology (CIST).
